The Visionary: Rama Yassin

RY

Rama Yassin

Syrian Moroccan Author, Architecture Student, and Founder

**ArchiLit** was born from Rama's pursuit of the **"fine line between writing and architecture."** An alumna of the Olive Writer creative program and a competition participant, she established this journal to foster an intellectual space where the discipline of design meets the freedom of expression. Her vision connects rigorous technical practice with unbridled artistic expression, celebrating both Moroccan intellectual heritage and global creative discourse.

Contribute to the Harmony.

We are currently accepting original articles, essays, fiction, and poetry that creatively link the principles of Structure (Architecture, Engineering, Design) with the art of Expression (Literature).
